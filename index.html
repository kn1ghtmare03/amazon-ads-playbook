Title

UAC Campaign Scaled Down

Optimization Playbook Troubleshooting
Problem Statement
What measures should be taken if campaigns stop spending or experience a steep scale-down?
Why is it Happening? / Things to Check
The Cost per in-app action is higher than the TCPA bid given to the campaign in the last 7-14 days. Check from which day did the scale down start.
Probable Solution
- Increase the TCPA by 10-15% every 5-7 days to test Incremental Cost per acquisition. - If in-app action is not recording, raise to support/Google rep.
